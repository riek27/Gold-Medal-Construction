About Our Company

Reliable construction, real estate and building material supply you can trust

Building With Integrity & Quality

Promise Building Company Limited is a trusted construction and real estate company providing professional building, fencing, property rental, and building material supply services. We are committed to delivering high-quality, durable and affordable construction solutions for individuals, companies and communities.

We specialize in building houses and commercial properties, selling land and houses, supplying blocks and aggregates, renting out residential and commercial properties, and designing modern buildings and flats.

Our experienced team ensures every project is handled with professionalism, reliability and attention to detail — from concept and design through to construction and completion.
