About Our Company

Building South Sudan's future through engineering excellence and reliable construction solutions

Building South Sudan's Future

Point Investment Company Limited is a premier provider of engineering, construction support, and supply services based in Juba, South Sudan. With years of experience in the region, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for both local and international clients.

Our company specializes in delivering high-quality solutions for infrastructure development, construction projects, and reliable supply chains. We understand the unique challenges and opportunities in South Sudan's growing economy and are committed to contributing to its development.

Our team consists of experienced professionals with deep knowledge of local conditions, regulations, and best practices in engineering and construction. We combine international standards with local expertise to deliver exceptional results for every project.
