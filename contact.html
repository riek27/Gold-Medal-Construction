Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What areas in South Sudan do you serve?

+

We are based in Juba and serve clients throughout South Sudan, including Central Equatoria, Upper Nile, Jonglei, Unity, and other states. We have completed projects in various regions across the country.

How quickly can you start a new project?

+

After initial consultation and project planning, we can typically mobilize within 2-4 weeks, depending on project scale and complexity. We maintain a ready team and equipment for timely project commencement.

Do you provide both design and construction services?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive services including engineering design, construction, and project management. We can handle projects from concept to completion or provide specific services based on your needs.

What types of construction materials do you supply?

+

We supply a wide range of construction materials including cement, steel, aggregates, roofing materials, plumbing and electrical supplies, finishing materials, and specialized equipment for construction projects.
